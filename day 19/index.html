GOA has two types of Courses and here they are:

  • Programming Courses
  • Design

Both are very good and if you learn one of them, you will be succesful person, so go join GOA, never too late! In Programming Courses, there are six kinds of groups and they are:

  • Group 012

  • Group 5
  • Group 6
